Warrior Life Hackathon

Join the first-ever internal hackathon focused on enhancing student life at the University of Waterloo. Students will collaborate to create innovative solutions that improve the campus experience for our community.

About the Hackathon

The Warrior Life Hackathon brings together undergraduate and graduate students from all faculties to tackle real challenges facing our campus community. Over 48 hours, teams will develop creative solutions for everything from campus navigation and study spaces to mental health resources and social connections.

What to Expect

  • 48-hour intensive development period
  • Mentorship from industry professionals and faculty
  • Workshops on design thinking and rapid prototyping
  • Networking opportunities with fellow students
  • Prizes and recognition for outstanding projects
  • Free meals and refreshments throughout the event

Challenge Categories

Campus Navigation & Accessibility

Help students navigate our complex campus more efficiently and make it more accessible for everyone.

Academic Support & Study Tools

Create tools that enhance learning, facilitate study groups, or improve academic planning.

Mental Health & Wellness

Develop solutions that support student mental health and promote campus wellness.

Campus Services & Resources

Improve access to dining, housing, recreation, and other essential campus services.

Community Building & Social Connection

Foster connections between students and strengthen our campus community.

Sustainability & Campus Life

Create solutions that promote environmental sustainability on campus.
