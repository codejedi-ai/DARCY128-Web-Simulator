Warrior Life Hackathon

Internal hackathon focused on enhancing student life at the University of Waterloo

About the Hackathon

The Warrior Life Hackathon is an internal University of Waterloo event where students collaborate to create innovative solutions that enhance the student experience on campus. From improving campus navigation to developing study group platforms, meal planning tools, and mental health resources - this hackathon focuses on building for our community, by our community.

Event Highlights

  • 48-hour intensive hackathon - Build, iterate, and create
  • $10,000 in prizes - Cash awards and tech gear
  • 200+ student participants - Connect with fellow Warriors
  • Industry mentorship - Guidance from professionals and alumni
  • Campus-focused challenges - Solve real UWaterloo problems
